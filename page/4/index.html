Jekyll markdown lists that don't start on 1.

This is just a short note to myself and anyone who encounters the same issue. While writing a post recently I wanted a numbered list that started with a number other than 1. Frustratingly, Jekyll does not support this. Jekyll always start lists with 1. I found this very inconvenient and tried to find a way around it. One solution is inline HTML.
